Other people's sites

"Do you want to exchange buttons?"

I'm very particular in whom I want to feature here. I like high quality sites with good content. Now, that's a bit vague, so I'll specify:

  • Your site is well-made at a technical level; it's responsive, accessible, has good contrast, images are optimized, etc.
  • It contains something more than just links to your socials, button wall, and an "about me" page with a list of all your mental illnesses (bonus points if self-diagnosed via TikTok).
  • My favorite websites are those that showcase appealing art, fanfiction, food recipes, or various technical guides. Those are some of the topics that I'm interested in, but the list is non-exhaustive.

If you think that you qualify and you'd like to reach out, you can email me at sqvrltastic@gmail.com, but if you don't get a reply, please don't take it personally.
