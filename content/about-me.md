I grew up in an age when everybody was still somewhat apprehensive of the internet and you were advised to never share your real name or photos online. Nowadays, it's the opposite and I hate how normalized oversharing on the internet is, to the point that if you don't have a social media account, people think you are some sort of serial killer, but I digress.
