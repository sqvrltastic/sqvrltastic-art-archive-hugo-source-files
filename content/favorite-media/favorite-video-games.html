--- title: Favorite games description: "List of my favorite video games" ---

I'm an avid PC gamer and I love a lot of old-school games with a dark and gritty atmosphere. I also tend to enjoy games that evoke a certain feeling of solitude and isolation that I find strangely comforting.

Honorable mention: Witcher 3, but only the soundtrack. Now, the game is good, but pretty overrated and has lots of issues, sucky combat and ubishit-tier map design for one. But the soundtrack? It's great, really. It's quite unique and every song is very recognizable. Furthermore, I love that it sounds distinctly slavic, instead of your average forgettable Hans Zimmer-esque generic fantasy soundtrack.
