--- title: Favorite anime and cartoons description: "List of my favorite animated films" date: 2026-01-01 ---

I rarely watch anime for the plot. I think in general we overestimate the importance of "plot" in film and other visual media. You can say that I mostly watch anime for the "vibes". A purely emotional experience, not tainted by "logic" or "story". I do really enjoy media with deep and complex characters though.

In general, I am of the opinion that art shouldn't really teach you anything per se or "convey a message", because that's boring and preachy. If you want to share your opinion on whatever subject, write an essay instead. Art is meant for exploration.

Oh and, I fucking hate shounen and FOTM slop, yeah.
